About Me

Hi👋🏻, I'm Irene, a backend engineer. My path here wasn't a straight line. I spent two years working in IT support and QA testing, which meant a lot of time digging into how systems behave, why things break, and what "working correctly" really means from a user's perspective. That experience shapes how I build software today.

Over the past couple of years I've focused on backend development with Python and FastAPI, building out three personal projects covering real estate, inventory management, and e-learning. Each one has full CRUD, role based access, JWT authentication, and cloud image storage, and each is deployed and live.

I work end to end, from designing database schemas with PostgreSQL and SQLAlchemy, to handling authentication, to deploying on cloud platforms. I'm also expanding into Node.js and TypeScript to broaden what I can build.

Currently open to backend engineering roles where I can contribute from my first day and keep growing.
